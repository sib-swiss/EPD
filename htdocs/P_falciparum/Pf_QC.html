Core promoter element analysis is performed in order to investigate the quality of the promoter collection. It leverages the preferential occurrence of certain DNA motifs at characteristic distances from the TSS. For P. falciparum, we optimized an initiator motif (Inr-pfa) that is found at the TSS of nearly half of the genes.

Inr-pfa: it is found at the TSS and shows great enrichment in EPDnew compared to ENSEMBL promoter collection.
