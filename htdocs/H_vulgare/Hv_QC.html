TATA box: this core promoter element is normally found around 30 bp upstream of the TSS in plants. The following plot shows that the EPDnew promoter collection has a more focused TATA-box distribution compared to RefSeq/UCSC annotation suggesting a precise TSS mapping in EPDnew.

Basic YR initiator (consensus sequence YR): R is found exatcly at the TSS.

Y-patch: It consists of an array of Y (C/T) nucleotides. The last position of the motif contains an R (A/G) nucleotide and corresponds exactly to the TSS. This motif, which primarily occurs in plants, was first described by Yamamoto et al. BMC Genomics, 2007.

GGCCCA motif (exact match): this hexamer is highly enriched in plant promoters between 150 to 50 bp upstream of the TSS.
